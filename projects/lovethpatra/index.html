Welcome!

LovethPatra Bright Future foundation is outlined for youth empowerment support, promote quality education, and encourage hope to the needy.

We costantly hope for a better tomorrow.

About Us

Lovethpatra Bright Future Foundation is a non charitable organization formed in 2017 in Los Angeles, California USA. Outlined to feed the homeless, restore hope to the needy, under privileged, orphans, transform lives, promote integrity, and self esteem.

We are determined to support and promote young and adults, taking them off the street and turning their passion into a reality. We hope to support youth empowerment, quality education, widows & widowers support, support the physically challenged, and the terminally sick individuals.

Our foreign mission aim is to cut down the death rates in African countries, Asians, Indian, and other under/ developing countries, by giving out donations; clothing, food, financial support, hospital equipment and educational study materials.

Our Values

Inspire hope and self esteem

Promote Love, Kindness, Faith, Compassion and self independence in the society.

Lead a positive mass movement in the community

Counsell, pray, motivate and add values to the less fortunate and the society at large.

Our Mission

Extend our generosity by distributing food, clothing and financial support to the needy, the homeless, the less privileged, the orphanage, support the widows/ widowers and the physically challenged individuals.
Build a healthy environment by combating hard drugs, human trafficking, gun violence and other anti social behaviors visible in our society.
Offer counseling and skills acquisition to curb the maniacs of violence and anti-social behaviors visible in our society.
Promote self independence by rendering physical and moral support to the unemployed.

Foreign Mission

Our foreign mission aimed to cut down the death rates in African countries, Asians, Indian, and other under/ developing countries, by giving out donations; clothing, food, financial support, hospital equipment and educational study materials.

We hope to organize sports competitions to elevate sport development and talent hunt in overseas, we also hope to enhance quality education by organizing seminars and quizzes competitions in schools.
